But they didn't believe it and chalked it up to fiction or worse, schizophrenia.
After this situation, friends teased Gosha all the time. Days passed, one day, one day Gosha went to the bathroom and slipped,
hitting his head on the toilet. He was unconscious for 1 hour, but still came to his senses, and very bad thoughts came to his mind about the society (his friends),
They say they don't understand him. The night passed, and the next day he went for a walk in the park, he met his friends, and they began to tease him again He decided to answer them harshly, "go fuck yourself!", later he was attracted, but he tried to give it, unsuccessfully. He lay in the snow for half an hour after the fight and thought how stupid society was and did not understand him.
He wanted to take revenge first on his friends for bullying him. The next day, he started to swing. After a month, he was no longer recognizable, he became smart, strong, understood how to travel through universes,
quantum physics. The hour of reckoning has come. When his friends were walking in a crowd, drinking harmful drinks and eating chips, he decided to take revenge on them,
when they approached him, they did not recognize him immediately, but then they began to call him a weirdo and strange, they began to tell him to move away from them,
after that, he immediately fucked his former friend. The rest of the crowd swooped down on him. Makhach began. Gosha studied martial arts,
at the same time, friends could only hit backhand, without following the technique. Gosha scattered everyone without difficulty, after which some beaten friends took items for self-defense,
to beat Gosha. Kurban took a stick and hit Gosha on the head. The stick broke in two, and the Kurban flew in. The rest of the friends ran away in fear, and Gosha told them after them, "Run, run, you won't escape anyway!".
After that, his friends ran into a friend's hut, they thought that Gosha would not find them, but Gosha knew where they were. Having knocked down the door, Gosha breaks into the hut, takes one of his friends and lifts him up,
holding his hands on his throat, at this time a second friend runs at Gosha with a mop (or something else), but Gosha takes hold of this object and breaks it into two parts, he released the lifeless body of the first friend and hit the second one with his foot,
the third one ran up to Gosha from behind, but Gosha knew this move, and drove his friend into the wall with a U-turn. Gosha took revenge, but it wasn't enough for him, he set fire to that hut and went home. He knew that he was fired up by the entrance cameras and decided,
